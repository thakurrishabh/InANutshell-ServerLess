Our Background

Who we are & What we dif?

The journey started when a group of grads took up the task of designing a cloud platform that can store and summarize documents & converting them into audio clips. A reason for this being increased engagement with audio and visual content than traditional reading. Moreover, there are times when we want to stay away from screens, so at these times there need be a way to consume those pile of documents with just voice commands. We want to develop a service which store, categorize, summarize and audio convert a given document and provide a seamless audio experience with Alexa.
